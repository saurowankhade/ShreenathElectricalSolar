About Us

Shreenath Electrical And Engineers Contractor


We, Shreenath Electrical and Engineers Contractor is established before 22 years in August 1998. We are one of the leading and prominent Electrical contractors in Washim District. The reason behind this successful journey is the binding with ‘Quality’ and ‘timely services’. The journey started as a labor contractor and with the passing time presently working as a Full Turnkey Contractor which includes Procurement of both Service and Material required for HT, LT Electric Line, DTC Work, Internal Electric Fittings and other Electrical Equipment unit Installations. Along with this Firm also started Manufacturing of Mild Steel Products which are required for HT, LT Electric Line and Distribution Transformer Structure. Also Firm is the only local body who take full turnkey contracts of Installations, Erection and Enersization of HT, LT Electric Line and DTC work. The Maintenance Contracts are also handled by firm which includes Transformers, HT, LT Electric Line. Currently, we are also a wholesaler Provider of Electric Material. The Main Client of Firm is MSEDCL, after that Private Organizations, Municipal Corporation, Minor Irrigation, PWD, Jilha Parishad etc. Firm Current Turnover is near about 5 Crore Rs. and its increasing day to day. In future Firm want to work in the Field of Solar Plant, Manufacturing and Maintenance of Distribution Transformers.
